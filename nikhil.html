How CAPS Session-booking works?

Session request

Select which kind of session you want to select. One-on-one(if you are a student) or Peer-learning ( if you are a faculty requesting for your class)

Fill the form

Fill the form providing the requested data. If you are a faculty, register first to request the Peer session.

Check the Emails

Once your request has been accepted , you will recieve an email from CAPS informing the date and volunteer details for the session.
